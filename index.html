About Us

We are an independent, family run, neighbourhood restaurant located in the community of Crouch End, North London. We are passionate about sustainably sourced seafood and our aim is to deliver the highest quality food and service in a relaxed, welcoming yet vibrant environment.Seafood fans will love our kitchen's offbeat and creative approach to ingredients. Oysters are firmly under the spotlight here at Lyon's, which a each variety paired with a particular garnish in order to "amplify and compliment their specific natural flavour".
